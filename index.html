How It Works

Three steps. Zero queues.

01
📱

Create Your Account

Sign up in minutes. Add your children's details, link your mobile money wallet, and set a secure PIN.

02
🏫

Choose Your School

Browse Konsi's directory of verified Zambian schools. Select your child's school, grade, and the term you're paying for.

03
✅

Pay & Get Receipt

Enter the amount, confirm with your PIN, and the payment goes directly to the school's bank account. Instant receipt issued.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🔒

PIN-Secured Payments

Every transaction is authorised with your personal 4-digit PIN. No payment goes through without your approval.

⚡

Instant Transfer

Funds move directly from your mobile wallet to the school's bank account in real time. No delays, no middlemen.

🧾

Digital Receipts

Every payment generates a timestamped reference number and receipt. Download or share it any time.

📊

Payment History

Track every fee payment across all your children and schools in one clean dashboard. Always know what's been paid.

🏫

School Dashboard

Schools get a live portal showing every incoming payment, with auto-reconciliation and Excel export for accounting.

🔔

Payment Reminders

Opt in to receive reminders before fee deadlines so your child never misses a term due to a late payment.
